== Description ==
Jerrynest is a theme based on the Writers. It remove some unnecessary settings for the blog (jerrynest.io). 
Original Description: Writers is a clean and professional responsive blog theme with focus on typography and content. It is SEO optimised for Google, meaning that it has clean & light code and loads fast. It is perfect for a journalist, author or blogger who is interested in creating a newspaper magazine website like Medium with focus on publishing high quality journalism & blogging. The design is flat, minimal and simple, it is responsive so it works on a mobile phone, tablets and desktop. So bloggers and publisher, it's time to get creative and start writing news about fitness, travel, business, beauty, food, lifestyle, wellness, apps, gaming, personal diary, affiliate marketing, personal journal, entertainment or whatever your readers want to read. Reading a story, article or post with SEO optimized content is easy with our elegant SEO friendly editorial theme. Writers has a sidebar where you can put your archive or other widgets. Enjoy creating your personal writing portfolio. Publish your first article today with our awesome & responsive blog theme!

== Installation ==
	
1. In your admin panel, go to Appearance > Themes and click the Add New button.
2. Click Upload and Choose File, then select the theme's .zip file. Click Install Now.
3. Click Activate to use your new theme right away.
